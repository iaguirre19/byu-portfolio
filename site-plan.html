Qualifications

My academic journey and work experience have played a pivotal role in shaping me into a programming professional. My academic background includes a strong foundation in Electronics and Communication Engineering acquired during my years at CDMX - Unitec from 2014 to 2017.

In addition to my engineering studies, I completed a comprehensive FullStack Development program at FreeCodeCamp, significantly enhancing my programming skills and enabling me to tackle more complex projects.

Currently, I'm dedicated to furthering my education and refining my expertise in software development at the Escuela de Programación y Desarrollo de Software de Platzi, where I continue to expand my knowledge and skills in this dynamic field.

My work experience has exposed me to diverse and challenging roles in the field:

During my time at Redline Telecommunications, I was involved in the design of metropolitan networks, gaining invaluable insights into network architecture and infrastructure.

As an Implementation Support Engineer at INT Intelligence and Telecom Technologies México SA de CV, I contributed to the successful implementation of telecom solutions, further enhancing my problem-solving abilities.

My role as a Sales Representative at Brighthaus LLC provided me with hands-on experience in sales and marketing, allowing me to bridge the gap between technology and business.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, my commitment to excellence and my unwavering passion for learning have consistently propelled me to tackle challenges effectively and deliver innovative solutions.

Portfolio

Projects are at the heart of my career and my passion. I've had the privilege of contributing to a variety of projects that showcase my skills and expertise. Here are three of the most noteworthy projects I've been a part of:

Project 1: Phone Book for California Auto Insurance Company This project involved the development of a comprehensive phone book system for a California-based auto insurance company. I played a pivotal role in creating a CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) system and a user login mechanism. Depending on their roles, users could access and manage company user information. This project demonstrated my ability to design and implement user-friendly systems for efficient data management.

Project 2: Human Resources Registration System Another significant project was the implementation of a Human Resources registration system. This project followed a uniform design scheme for consistency. I contributed to the development and maintenance of this system, ensuring a streamlined and efficient HR process. My involvement in this project highlighted my attention to detail and commitment to improving business processes.

Project 3: Technology Company Landing Page I had the pleasure of working on a landing page for a technology company based in California. This project allowed me to showcase my web design and development skills. The landing page was designed to create a compelling online presence for the company, emphasizing their services and mission. It was an exciting opportunity to fuse creativity with technology.

These projects represent just a fraction of my portfolio, and I'm eager to share more about my contributions and achievements on my website.
